Zero Point Five Love (Chinese: 0.5的爱情) is a 2014 Chinese romance film directed by GengXiao. It was released on September 12, 2014.[1]

§Cast[edit]

  • Purba Rgyal
  • Jessie
  • Jie Xi
  • Yue Ming Li

§Reception[edit]

It has earned ¥0.54 million at the Chinese box office.[1]
