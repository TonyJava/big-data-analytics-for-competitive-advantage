Zombie Genocide is a 1993 Irish zombie film directed by Darryl Sloan. It was edited in-camera, that is without the use of any external editing systems or programs whatsoever. It is recognized as the first zombie film to emerge from Ireland.

Currently it is only available from the Midnight Pictures website on DVD-R.
